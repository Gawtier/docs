Forest Admin is compatible with AWS's SAML 2.0 application. The platform allows organizations to enable Single Sign-On through AWS IAM Identity Center.

## Accessing Organization Settings

To configure SSO or SCIM:

1. Click your **Gravatar** in the bottom-left corner
2. Select **Organization Settings**
3. Navigate to the **SSO** or **SCIM** tab

## Prerequisites

<Warning>
This feature may not be available on all plans. Check the [pricing page](https://www.forestadmin.com/pricing) for more information, or [contact us](https://www.forestadmin.com/contact) to enable it for your organization.
</Warning>

## Configuration Steps

Follow AWS's detailed guide titled "Set up your own SAML 2.0 application" to enable SSO from your AWS IAM Identity Center.

### Required Settings

| Setting | Description | Value |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Application ACS URL** | Receives the SAML response; use for Recipient and Destination URLs | `https://api.forestadmin.com/api/saml/callback` |
| **Application SAML audience** | Globally unique identifier Forest Admin provides (Unique User ID) | `forestadmin-OrganizationName` |
| **Relay State** (Optional) | Required only for IDP-initiated login scenarios | `{"organizationName": "<OrganizationName>", "destinationUrl": "organization.projects"}` |

## Troubleshooting

When encountering issues, verify the following:

- Confirm all information accuracy, including endpoints and certificate expiration dates
- Ensure the `nameID` configured on your Identity Provider matches the email address used on Forest Admin accounts
